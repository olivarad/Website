Update 1

Published on: January 3, 2024

As part of the initial update, I have incorporated features that allow the player to perform several actions. These actions include walking, running, crouching, shielding, dashing, and wall sliding. Each of these actions is accompanied by corresponding animations, and additional animations have been implemented to depict the player's descent and ascent.
